Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing gloves and protective headgear, punch each other with their fists in a boxing ring. The objective of the sport is to score more points by landing punches on the opponent's head or upper body than the opponent scores on the boxer. A match typically consists of three-minute rounds with one-minute breaks in between.Boxing has a rich history and has been a popular sport for centuries. It was first introduced as a competitive sport in the ancient Olympic Games in Greece and has since evolved into a professional sport with organized competitions around the world.
